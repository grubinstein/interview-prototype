Gameplay

When a wild appears on screen, it will expand to fill its reel. After wins are awarded, all reels that are not filled with wilds will spin again. Wins are awarded again and, if a new wild has landed, the reels without wilds spin again. Respins continue until a spin occurs in which no new wilds land. This makes for a maximum of 3 respins, which would result in the the middle three reels filled with wilds for a huge win!

To see a big win with multiple respins paste "0,0,0,0,0,0,14,69" into the Gaffe text box below the game and click spin.
